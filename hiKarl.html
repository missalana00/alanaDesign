Overview & Roles








Overview

Hi Karl is a virtual concierge that offers personalized event recommendations directly from Facebook messenger. You can ask Hi Karl what’s happening in town, who’s playing at a specific venue, or where to hear some good #indie tunes, and Karl will get back to you in seconds!


Roles

UX Researcher & Designer

Framing the Challenge







How Might We?

Drawing upon IDEO's 'Frame Your Design' challenge, I like to begin the discovery process by framing the challenge in a way that drives toward the ultimate impact, allows for a variety of solutions, and takes context into consideration.
