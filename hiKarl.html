Case Study




Hi Karl

Your evening genie for personalized event recommendations



Overview & Roles




Overview

Hi Karl is a virtual concierge that offers personalized music event recommendations directly from Facebook messenger via an easy, natural language based interface.

By connecting with users' Spotify accounts, behavioral and geographical data, Hi Karl ensures ease of discovery of relevant, nearby music events.

You can ask Karl what’s happening in town, who’s playing at a specific venue, or where to hear some good #indie tunes, and Karl will get back to you in seconds.


Roles

UX Researcher & Designer


Project

With this project, an existing design had been launched in select cities. In hopes of better serving the target audience and improving the overall user experience, my role was to direct a research validated redesign that would accomplish these goals.

Framing the Challenge




How Might We?

Following a strong project kickoff meeting with stakeholders, I began the discovery process by framing the project challenge in the most human way I could. What followed was a refining of the challenge into a problem statement that would drive toward the ultimate impact, allow for a variety of solutions, and take context into consideration.







Problem statement study. Used post-it notes to quickly jot down ideas, consider all statements together, and easily remove insufficent answers and insert better ones where necessary .



Understanding the Ultimate Impact






What's the Top of the Mountain Here?

To gain a firm grasp of what I was trying to accomplish and how success might later be measured, I sought to understand the company's desired ultimate impact. A wise design instructor I had phrased the consideration like this:



"How will you know you've achieved everything you can imagine and then it's 'all down hill from here'?"

Building a Provisional Persona




Who's Our Target?

To begin the design process with our true target audience in mind, I created user interview guides for strategic customer discovery and conducted qualitative research with seven prospective users to generate a provisional persona encompassing personality, demographics, key behaviors, common goals, frequent frustrations, and psychological motivations.
